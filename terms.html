Odeskwork offers you a safe & reliable payment system

Odeskwork payment system is integrated with the Credits – a helpful feature for the admin to create a trustworthy marketplace. Currently, Credits is working with PayPal, Bank Transfer and Credits to hold money as a neutral third party in the transaction until the service or good is delivered.

Once you enable system, you – the admin of the site will become a contractor between the freelancer and employer. Whenever an employer accepts a bid, he has to send money including budget of that project and commission to the admin. Admin will keep this money until the project is completed.

In case of dispute, admin will play a role as judge to make the final decision to refund money to the employer or send the payment for the freelancer based on their reasons. Hence, Odeskwork can give you the ability to ensure the safety of each transaction and the benefits of your users.
